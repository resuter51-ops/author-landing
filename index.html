About the Author

Robert E. Suter is a 73-year-old author writing from a nursing home in New Jersey. Living with CIDP (Chronic Inflammatory Demyelinating Polyneuropathy), he turned to storytelling — proving that it's never too late to chase a passion. His five books span heartwarming fiction, small-town drama, and a three-part adventure series, all written with the honesty, humor, and heart that come from a life fully lived.
